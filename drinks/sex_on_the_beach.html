Sex On the beach

This is one of my favorite drinks, is tasty, sweet and easy to make. and best of all it never's go wrong.

Ingredients

  1. 1 1/2oz of vodka
  2. 1/2oz of peach schnapps
  3. 2oz of orange juice
  4. 2oz of cranberry juice
  5. Ice

Instructions

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice.
  2. Pour the vodka, peach schnapps, orange juice, and cranberry juice into the glass.
  3. Stir well.
  4. Garnish with an orange slice.
  5. And, enjoy...
