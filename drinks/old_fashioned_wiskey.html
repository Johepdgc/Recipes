Wiskey

The wiskey is better to drink with just ice or a little bit of water, but it can be quite strong, so this is a cocktail that doenst ruin the experience completely.

Ingredients

  1. 2 ounces of wiskey
  2. 1 teaspoon of sugar
  3. Club soda to top
  4. 1 orange peel
  5. Ice

Preparation

  1. Put the ice in a glass
  2. Add the wiskey
  3. Add the sugar
  4. Add the club soda
  5. Add the orange peel
  6. Enjoy!
