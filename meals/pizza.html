The pizza

A pizza, we cant dennie its always delicious, and yes you can take a delivery or go to the pizza place. but belive in me, making it its easy, cheap and fun!

Ingredients

  1. 2 to 1/4 teaspoon of yeast
  2. 1 and 1/3 cups of warm water
  3. Flour
  4. 1/4 cup of olive oil
  5. Salt
  6. Sugar
  7. Tomato sauce
  8. 1/2 cup of mozzarella cheese
  9. Pepperoni

Preparation

  1. Put the yeast in the warm water and let it rest for 5 minutes
  2. Put the flour in a bowl and add the yeast and water mix
  3. Add the olive oil, salt and sugar
  4. Mix it all together until you get a dough
  5. Let it rest for 30 minutes
  6. Preheat the oven to 200Â°C
  7. Put the dough in a pizza tray
  8. Add the tomato sauce
  9. Add the mozzarella cheese
  10. Add the pepperoni
  11. Put it in the oven for 15 minutes
  12. Enjoy! And if your not conviced yet, then call your pizza delivery, it does the work!
